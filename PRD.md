# 5 app ideas pulled from real Reddit complaints - Pet Medication Tracker

## Track

## Problem
Pet owners with animals on multiple medications miss doses and confuse meds, and human health apps don't map well to pet schedules.

Proposed MVP: A simple mobile web app where users can add pet profiles, input medication schedules (e.g. 2x a day, every 12 hours), and get SMS or email reminders. It should include photo uploads of the pill bottle to prevent confusion.

## Why now
A medication tracker for pets. found 22+ threads from pet owners with animals on multiple medications. they're missing doses, confusing which med is which...
